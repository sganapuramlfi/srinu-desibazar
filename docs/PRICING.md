## â“ FAQs

### Can I change plans later?
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade anytime from your billing portal.

### Do you offer refunds?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on annual plans. Monthly subscriptions are non-refundable but can be cancelled anytime.

### What happens to my data if I cancel?
Your data remains accessible for 30 days after cancellation. You can export all data from your account settings.

### Can I get more storage/credits without upgrading?
Yes! Add-on packs are available:
- Storage: $10/month per 5GB
- AI Credits: $20/month per 1,000 credits

### Is there a setup fee?
No setup fees for any plan. Start using the platform immediately.

### Can I use my own domain?
Yes, custom domains are available on Enterprise plans. Premium plans can use subdomain (yourbusiness.desibazaar.com.au).
